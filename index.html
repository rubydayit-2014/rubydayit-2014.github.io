Conference Venue

RubyDay 2014 will be held on 26/09/2014 in the beautiful location of H-Farm Ventures - Roncade (Treviso)

H-Farm is settled in Ca' Tron estate, a beautiful countryside bounded by the Sile river, located between Treviso and Venice province, just 5 minutes from Venice Airport. H-FARM is very easy to reach either by car, train or from the Venice International Airport.

If you are coming by train, check the private transfer service from the Venezia Mestre station to H-Farm and back.

Via Sile, 41 - 31056 Roncade
